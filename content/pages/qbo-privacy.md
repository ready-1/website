# QuickBooks Integration Privacy Notice

_Effective May 4, 2026._

This notice applies to Ready-1 LLC's internal QuickBooks Online integration.

The integration is used for Ready-1 business administration. It is not a public consumer application.

## Information accessed

Depending on the approved workflow, the integration may access QuickBooks Online information such as:

- company profile information;
- customer and vendor records;
- estimates, invoices, payments, items, and account-related records;
- transaction metadata needed to reconcile Ready-1 operational records with QuickBooks.

## How information is used

Ready-1 uses this information to support internal accounting, estimating, billing preparation, reporting, and operational planning.

Ready-1 does not sell QuickBooks data.

Ready-1 does not use QuickBooks data for unrelated advertising or public marketing services.

## Sharing

QuickBooks data is shared only as needed for Ready-1 operations, professional services, legal compliance, accounting support, or authorized technical support.

## Storage and access

Access is limited to authorized Ready-1 personnel and operators. Credentials and tokens should be stored in controlled Ready-1 systems, not in public repositories or public documents.

## Retention

Ready-1 retains QuickBooks-related information as needed for business records, accounting obligations, troubleshooting, and operational continuity.
